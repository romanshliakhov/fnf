Description

Now you have the opportunity to play your favorite FNF game with your friend, neighbor or just an acquaintance. Many favorite antagonists are available to choose from in this mod, including: Dear Mommy and Daddy, Whitty, Skid, Pump, Agoti, Ruv, Tankman and others. In general, everyone can find their favorite here, with whom they can perform many musical compositions. And also there are difficulty modes to choose from, from very simple to insanely complex, in which the arrows fly at incredible speed. Create a game for yourself and defeat your opponents.

By choosing a hero, difficulty of the level and track, you enter into battle with your friend. By the way, all these features are also available to another player. Each player must watch their half of the screen, along which the arrows run. When the flying symbols coincide with the gray outlines, click the corresponding key. For each correct click, players will earn points, which at the end of the game will show which of you is more capable, more agile and attentive, and who becomes the winner.
